Quick Help
🔑 I forgot my password
If you\'re an owner: use your recovery code on the login page. If you\'re staff: ask your admin to reset it from Team Management.
📦 My data isn\'t syncing
Check the sync dot in the top bar. Green = synced, red = offline. Tap it to open Database Sync and check your Firebase connection.
👥 How do I add staff?
Tell your staff to download the app and sign up as “Staff” using your exact business name. You\'ll get a notification to approve them in Team Management.
💰 How do I add my business currency?
Go to Sidebar → Business Settings → Currency. Choose from USD, LRD, EUR, GBP, NGN and more.
